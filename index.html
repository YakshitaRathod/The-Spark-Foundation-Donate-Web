Help feed poor children

95.1 million children have been deprived of midday meals during the pandemic, adding to the child malnutrition load. Join this mission and provide monthly nutrition kits to ensure poor children are not malnourished. The kits comprise immunity-boosting formulas, multi-vitamins, iron and protein rich food, including dates, Horlicks, chickpea, ragi flour, etc. Just ₹1,000/month will safeguard one child from malnutrition.

Donate A Blanket. Save A Life

Every winter, The Spark Foundation runs a Blanket Donation Campaign, where we provide high quality warm blankets along with dry ration and dignity kits to the underprivileged living in the open on the streets. We try our best to reach out to people in Delhi NCR and other parts of north India. Donate a warm blanket and save the life of the homeless.

Donate Food for Poor Patients and their Families outside Hospitals

Your small contribution will support us to feed poor patients and their family members outside hospitals. Majority of the underprivileged patients admitted in Government hospitals are those who come from nearby states are often poor, in several cases family members decide to stay hungry to save money.
